RECIPE homemade pizza

enjoy classic pizza with simple ingredients

use bread flour this delivers a delicious crust

with enough dough this recipe is ideal for planning leftovers

my favourite

ingredients

-->
  1. 1 cup of warm water
  2. 1 package teaspoons active dry yeast
  3. 3 cups bread flour
  4. 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  5. 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  6. 1 teaspoon sugar

how to cook

  1. preheat the baking sheet
  2. divide the dough into 2
  3. prep the toppings
  4. flatten the dough into round
  5. brush with olive olive
  6. spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  7. slide the pizza into the over
  8. bake

serving

serve with prefered beverage and enjoy your pizza
